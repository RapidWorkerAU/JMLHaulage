Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

JML Hotshots is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when you use our website.

Information We Collect

The only personal information we currently collect through this website is your email address when you use the freight estimate calculator. We do not collect sensitive information, payment details, or identification documents through the site.

How We Use Your Email

We use your email address to send you the estimate you request and to follow up about your freight enquiry. We do not sell or rent your email to third parties.

Sharing Your Information

We only share your information when required to deliver our services, comply with the law, or protect our legal rights. If we engage service providers to help operate the website or communicate with you, they are required to protect your information.

Retention

We keep your email address only as long as needed to provide your estimate and maintain our records, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Analytics

We do not currently use cookies or analytics that identify you personally. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

Your Choices

You can contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your email address from our records.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at jarrad@jmlegg.com.au.
